# Ark of Osiris Strategy Planner

Plan and coordinate your alliance's 30v30 Ark of Osiris battles.

## Overview

Ark of Osiris is a 30v30 cross-server battle mode where alliances fight to control structures and score points. The Strategy Planner helps you:

- Assign players to roles (Structure, Altar, Shrine teams)
- Position players on the battle map
- Generate shareable strategy guides

## Using the Planner

### Step 1: Team Assignment

Organize your 30 players into specialized teams:

| Team | Focus | Recommended |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Structure** | Control main structures | Strong fighters, rally leaders |
| **Altar** | Capture and hold altars | Mobile, tanky marches |
| **Shrine** | Shrine rotation | Fast cavalry |
| **Defense** | Protect captured objectives | Garrison specialists |

### Step 2: Map Positioning

Use the interactive map to:
- Drag and drop players to positions
- Mark rally points
- Indicate movement routes

### Step 3: Share Strategy

Click **Copy Strategy** to generate a text summary you can paste into:
- Discord channels
- In-game alliance chat
- Line/WhatsApp groups

## Tips for Success

### Pre-Battle
- Set up rallies on key structures early
- Assign backup leaders for each objective
- Coordinate garrison commanders

### During Battle
- Communicate shrine rotations
- Call out enemy movements
- Adapt to enemy strategy

### Common Mistakes
- Spreading too thin across objectives
- No backup plan for failed rallies
- Poor communication during shrine phase

## Battle Map Reference

The standard Ark of Osiris map includes:

- **4 Main Structures** - High points, require rallies
- **4 Altars** - Medium points, can be captured solo
- **Shrines** - Rotating objectives, time-limited

Focus your strongest fighters on structures while maintaining altar control.

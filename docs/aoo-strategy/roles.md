# Team Roles

Every player in Ark of Osiris has specific responsibilities. Understanding your role ensures coordinated team play.

## Role Types

### Rally Leader

**Symbol:** Crown icon

**Responsibilities:**
- Command rallies on assigned buildings
- Call teleport timing for zone
- Coordinate offensive pushes
- Make strategic decisions for zone

**Requirements:**
- High troop count (50M+ power recommended)
- Multiple maxed rally commanders
- Discord/voice chat access
- Experience leading rallies

**Phase Assignments:**
| Phase | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Rush | Rush obelisk or prepare rally |
| Secure | TP first wave, rally structures |
| Ark | Rally shrines/altars, or Ark |
| Hold | Defend, rally only on call |

### Teleport 1st Wave

**Symbol:** Lightning bolt

**Responsibilities:**
- Teleport immediately when leaders call
- Join first rallies
- Establish early garrison presence
- Support rally leaders

**Requirements:**
- Fast response time
- Teleport items ready
- Strong garrison troops

### Teleport 2nd Wave

**Symbol:** Double lightning

**Responsibilities:**
- Teleport after initial rallies fire
- Reinforce newly captured buildings
- Fill garrison gaps
- Backup for first wave

**Requirements:**
- Teleport items ready
- Infantry-focused army

### Hold Obelisks

**Symbol:** Shield

**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain obelisk garrisons
- Never leave unless replaced
- Call for help if attacked
- Priority defense target

**Requirements:**
- Strong garrison commanders
- Patient playstyle
- Good communication

### Garrison

**Symbol:** Castle

**Responsibilities:**
- Fill and maintain building garrisons
- Rotate between buildings as needed
- Support wherever troops needed
- Never leave buildings empty

**Requirements:**
- Infantry troops preferred
- Garrison commanders
- Team awareness

### Conquer

**Symbol:** Sword

**Responsibilities:**
- Front-line building capture
- Quick strikes on enemy territory
- Support rally pushes
- Fast response to opportunities

**Requirements:**
- Cavalry-focused army
- Aggressive playstyle
- Quick reaction time

### Farm

**Symbol:** Wheat

**Responsibilities:**
- Gather resources for alliance points
- Stay near assigned zone
- Support defense when needed
- Lower priority than combat

**Requirements:**
- Gathering commanders
- Patience
- Awareness of battle state

## Role Distribution

Recommended distribution for a 30-player team:

| Role | Count | % of Team |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Rally Leader | 3-4 | 10-13% |
| Teleport 1st | 6-8 | 20-27% |
| Teleport 2nd | 6-8 | 20-27% |
| Hold Obelisks | 4 | 13% |
| Garrison | 4-6 | 13-20% |
| Conquer | 2-4 | 7-13% |
| Farm | 2-4 | 7-13% |

## Zone Assignments

### Zone 1 (Blue - Lower/Left)

**Buildings:**
- Obelisk (Left)
- Outpost of Iset 1
- Desert Altar (Left)
- Shrine of Life (Left)

**Typical Composition:**
- 1 Rally Leader
- 10-12 Support players
- 2-3 Gatherers

### Zone 2 (Orange - Center)

**Buildings:**
- Ark
- Central altars
- Shrines (rotation)

**Typical Composition:**
- 1-2 Rally Leaders (Ark specialists)
- 4-6 Elite garrison troops

### Zone 3 (Purple - Upper/Right)

**Buildings:**
- Obelisk (Upper)
- Outpost of Iset 2 & 3
- Sky Altar (Right)
- Shrine of War (Right)

**Typical Composition:**
- 1-2 Rally Leaders
- 10-12 Support players
- 2-3 Conquerors

## Multi-Role Players

Many players have multiple tags:

**Rally Leader + Teleport 1st:**
- Rushes to position
- Sets up rallies immediately

**Garrison + Hold Obelisks:**
- Primary defender
- Moves between critical buildings

**Conquer + Teleport 2nd:**
- Quick capture after initial wave
- Flexible offense/defense

## Substitutes

Substitute players (Team 0):
- Ready to fill any role
- Join if main player unavailable
- Should know multiple positions
- Stay online during match

## Communication

### Required Callouts

| Situation | What to Say |
|-----------|-------------|
| Building attacked | "[Building] under attack - need help!" |
| Enemy rally | "Rally incoming on [Building]" |
| Building captured | "[Building] captured, need garrison" |
| Ready for teleport | "Ready to TP" |
| Low troops | "Low troops, need replacement" |

### Discord Voice

Rally leaders should be in voice chat for:
- Real-time coordination
- Quick decision making
- Emergency response
- Strategy adjustments

# Equipment Scanner

Build your gear inventory by scanning equipment screenshots.

## Features

- **Auto-Detection** - Matches equipment from 45+ items database
- **Rarity Recognition** - Normal, Advanced, Elite, Epic, Legendary
- **Stats Extraction** - Attack, defense, health, and special bonuses
- **Set Tracking** - Identifies equipment set bonuses

## Equipment Types

The scanner recognizes all equipment slots:
- **Weapons** - Sword, bow, staff, etc.
- **Helmets** - Various head armor
- **Chest** - Body armor pieces
- **Gloves** - Hand protection
- **Boots** - Footwear
- **Accessories** - Rings, necklaces, etc.

## How to Use

1. Screenshot your equipment details screen
2. Upload to the Equipment Scanner
3. Review detected equipment and stats
4. Adjust rarity or stats if needed
5. Save to your inventory

## Stats Tracked

- Attack bonus (%)
- Defense bonus (%)
- Health bonus (%)
- March speed
- Skill damage
- Counterattack damage
- Special abilities
